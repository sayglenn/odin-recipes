Cookies

Fresh Cookies for All!

A fine dish for all, dessert for kids!

Ingredients

  • Melted Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • White Sugar
  • Cornstarch
  • Egg Yolk
  • Baking Soda
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps to Make

  1. Whisk the ingredients
  2. Cover and chill the dough
  3. Put the dough on a baking sheet
  4. Bake the cookies in the oven
  5. Take out and serve
