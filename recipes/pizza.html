Delicious Pizza to Feast On!

A hearty meal for everyone!

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps to Make

  1. Make the dough and knead it
  2. Let the dough sit and rise
  3. Take the dough out and punch and shape it
  4. Put all the toppings on the pizza
  5. Bake the pizza and serve
